Discover the Mysteries of Wilpattu

Wilpattu National Park, with its sprawling landscapes and diverse ecosystems, is a sanctuary for numerous species. Explore the wonders of this untamed wilderness and witness the beauty of nature in its rawest form.

  • Home to a rich variety of flora and fauna, Wilpattu boasts a unique and vibrant ecosystem.
  • Roam the vast expanses and encounter fascinating wildlife, from majestic elephants to elusive leopards.
  • Immerse yourself in the symphony of birdsong as you explore the park's diverse bird species.
  • Feel the pulse of nature as you witness the ancient Wilpattu reservoirs, essential lifelines for the park's inhabitants.

Tailor-Made Adventures in Wilpattu

Thrilling Safaris

Avian Paradise

Meet the Inhabitants of Wilpattu

Sri Lankan Leopard (Panthera pardus kotiya)

The apex predator of Wilpattu, known for its elusive nature and striking coat patterns.

Indian Peafowl (Pavo cristatus)

Witness the vibrant display of peafowls, the colorful gems of Wilpattu's avian population.

Asian Elephant (Elephas maximus)

Observe the gentle giants roaming the landscapes, a symbol of Wilpattu's ecological richness.
